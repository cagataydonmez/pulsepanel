# PulsePanel Product

PulsePanel is a native Apple ecosystem utility that lets an iPhone run trusted local commands on a Mac. It is not a remote desktop, trackpad replacement, cloud automation platform, or hardware-deck clone.

## Users

- Mac power users who repeatedly launch apps, websites, Shortcuts, and hotkeys.
- Creators, presenters, developers, students, and desk workers who want common Mac actions one tap away.
- Privacy-conscious users who prefer local-network tools with no account requirement.

## Product Principles

- Local first: the command path stays on the user's local network for MVP.
- Trust is visible: pairing, connection, and permission states are always clear.
- The dashboard is the product: the iPhone opens into useful tiles, not marketing.
- Native beats decorative: SwiftUI/AppKit conventions, SF Symbols, and platform behavior are preferred over custom novelty controls.
- Advanced Mac control is gated: Accessibility-based window control stays behind explicit permissions and future modules.

## Anti-References

- Do not copy Stream Deck, BetterTouchTool, Unified Remote, Kommand, ReMac, Wake PC, or Unyx branding, UI, wording, or visual identity.
- Do not claim "full Mac control" in MVP.
- Do not imply cloud sync or remote access outside the local network.
